Emergency Dental Services

Almost everyone needs a rush visit to the dentist at some point in their life. It could be that a filling has come out or a crown has come off, exposing the sensitive nerve of your tooth to heat, cold and air. A sports accident could damage a tooth or perhaps knock it out completely. Or the emergency could be a toothache that shows up suddenly to ruin your day.

When a problem like this shows up, call our office as soon as possible. We will find out what the problem is and work you into our schedule as soon as possible. We’ll help make you more comfortable and prevent further damage. Then we can schedule you for a regular visit to our office to complete any repairs needed. We understand that accidents and surprises do happen so please don’t delay calling when an emergency comes up.

No matter what your dental needs, Albania Dental is ready to help.
